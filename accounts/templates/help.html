About Us

We are a small group of NYU graduate students looking to provide NYC street accessibility data to individuals with mobility issues or visual impairments.


FAQ

How do I report an inaccessible ramp/signal pole?

On the left-hand side of the main page click "report issue" on the card whose location you want to report. Then fill out the issue and description options and click "submit report" to notify us.

How do I save frequently visited locations?

For each location card on the left hand side of the main page you can click the star in the top right corner of the card to add the location to your favorites. Once the star turns yellow it means it has been added to your favorites.
